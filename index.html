Late 2017–Early 2018

Kount - Fraud Prevention Solutions

Product Design

At Kount I was the sole designer. Working with the Product, Engineering, and Customer Success teams — and in collaboration with executive stakeholders — I was charged with redesigning the manual review workflows for fraud analysts in our Enterprise product, Kount Complete. The pre-release product was revealed in 2018 a the annual Kount Summit in Orland Florida only months after the redesign project began.

During the project, I discovered a passion for analyzing UX metrics, creating user feedback loops, and creating design systems.
